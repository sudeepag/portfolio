Preview

Vision

Vision in an app conceptualized at HackMIT that gives vision to the visually impaired and blind, by changing the way they navigate and interact with their environment. With the help of image recognition and OCR technology, users are able to identify objects (and get a description read out to them), listen to documents, and even get translated documents. Vision makes use of the Clarifai API, as well as the Microsoft Azure and Translator APIs.

Soundsafe

iOS Development, Hardware, Hackathons

Preview

Soundsafe

Soundsafe is a smart, modular electromagnetic locking system that is activated by matching a series of randomly generated sounds with varying frequeuncies. This sound frequency analysis is done using a discrete Hartley transform algorithm. The physical lock is made up of an Arduino Uno Board with a microphone for frequency charactization, a wifi shield, and an electromagnetic lock. It interacts with an iOS application that manages the user's locks and allows them to lock and unlock locks, as well as pair new locks.
